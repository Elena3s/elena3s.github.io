Charts / Interim points

At the age of 40 many Instacart customers transition from <200K to <300K income. The income ceiling also jumps up from 400K to 600K at the same age line of 40.

Results

The profile category "Parent over 30" (and below 65, with dependents) is by far the most numerous one. (NB: Some profiles may overlap.)

Region 3 has the most customers and almost doubles both High and Low spenders when compared to Region 1. Regions 2 and 4 are hanging somewhere in between.
